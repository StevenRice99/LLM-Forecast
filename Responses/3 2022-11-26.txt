Title: COVID-19 pandemic's impact on clinical psychiatric diagnoses in New York City
Source: Nature.com

The article discusses the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on clinical psychiatric diagnoses in New York City. Key findings include:

Source: CBC.ca

 1,800 patients are deemed to need an "alternate level of care" and are on a wait-list for long-term care.
