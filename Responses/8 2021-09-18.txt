4. Unvaccinated ICU patients: The fourth article highlights the pressure on local hospitals due to unvaccinated individuals being admitted to ICUs, leading to cancelled surgeries and elective procedures.
5. Breakthrough infections: The final three articles discuss breakthrough infections among fully vaccinated individuals, particularly among older adults with underlying health conditions.
