 Reinstating mask mandates in schools 
This suggestion comes from Article 4, which highlights the potential benefits of reinstating mask mandates in schools to mitigate the transmission of COVID-19 and other respiratory viruses. Experts warn about a potential "tridemic" this fall due to the circulation of RSV, influenza, and new COVID-19 variants, making masking an effective measure to reduce the spread of these illnesses, especially among vulnerable individuals.
