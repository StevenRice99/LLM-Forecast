Title: Ontario will no longer give AstraZeneca COVID-19 vaccine as 1st dose due to blood clot risk
Publisher: CBC.ca
Posted: 4 days ago
The province of Ontario has paused the rollout and administration of first doses of the AstraZeneca vaccine due to an increase in cases of a rare blood clotting syndrome (VITT) linked to the vaccine.
As of May 8, there were over 853,000 doses of AstraZeneca administered with a rate of VITT of roughly 1 per 100,000 doses administered.
The article mentions that COVID-19 case numbers are decreasing in Ontario, but does not provide specific hospitalization data.
