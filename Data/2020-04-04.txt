Title: Suspected, confirmed COVID-19 patients filling roughly 1 in 4 Ontario ICU beds
Publisher: CBC.ca
Posted: 5 days ago
Confirmed and suspected COVID-19 cases account for roughly one out of every four patients currently in Ontario's intensive care units. The number of confirmed cases has doubled every two days over the past week, while the number of suspected cases has not increased as rapidly. ICU occupancy rate is 76%, leaving around 500 beds available. A group of Toronto-based health scientists predict that demand for ICU beds from COVID-19 patients will peak in early-to-mid-April at more than 700 patients.
