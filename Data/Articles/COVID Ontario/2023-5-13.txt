Title: Study of Hospitalizations Shows COVID-19 Vaccine Benefit
Publisher: Medscape
Trusted: False
Posted: 5 days ago
The study analyzed 1.513 million hospital admissions in Canada over 2 years, including 51,679 adult and 4035 pediatric admissions for COVID-19.
Unvaccinated patients were up to 15 times more likely to die from COVID-19 than fully vaccinated patients.
Hospitalizations reached a peak of 14,461 during wave five (Dec 26, 2021 - March 19, 2022), with ICU admissions peaking at 2164 during wave four (July 1 - Dec 25, 2021).
The proportion of adults admitted to the ICU was significantly lower during waves five and six, with an odds ratio of 0.35.
The study highlights the benefit of vaccinations in protecting against severe COVID-19 outcomes, particularly in adults.
