VE was lower among older adults (53.3%) compared to working age adults.
Pfizer-BioNTech's BNT162b2 mRNA vaccine and Moderna's mRNA-1273 vaccine have been shown to be highly effective against severe COVID-19 in various studies, with VE ranging from 70 to 90% among older adults.
