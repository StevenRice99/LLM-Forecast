Article 1 of 6
Title: Validating hospital data for research and surveillance
Publisher: Health e-News
Posted: 1 day ago
The article confirms the validity of hospital diagnostic codes (ICD code U07.1) used to identify SARS-CoV-2 infections in patient data in Canada. The study found that 3.7% of hospital admissions were confirmed to have SARS-CoV-2 infection, and the results support the use of administrative hospitalization data for identifying COVID-19 cases.

Article 6 of 6
Title: Eating disorder hospitalizations for young men surged 416% in nearly 2 decades: report - National
Publisher: Global News
Posted: 5 days ago
The study found a 416% increase in eating disorder hospitalizations among young men in Ontario over 17 years.
This trend was observed across all age groups, with the largest increases for female patients.
The study suggests that existing eating disorder programs should adapt to address this trend and ensure effective care.
However, the article does not provide any direct information on COVID-19 hospitalizations in Ontario.
