NDP leader Andrea Horwath is calling on the Ontario government to provide direct financial support to households so they can pay rent and make it illegal to evict tenants during the COVID-19 pandemic.
Health-Care Worker's Sacrifice 
A Toronto health-care worker, Marci Halls, sent her young son to live with his grandparents in London, Ont., to protect him from the virus.
She documented her difficult decision on Instagram and hopes it will help people understand the sacrifices made by health-care workers.
Restaurant Industry's Plea for Help 
Canadian restaurant owners and food service workers have started a campaign called OneTable to secure federal support.
They are calling for immediate financial relief, protection from being locked out of their locations, and help to reopen and rehire employees once anti-coronavirus measures are eased.
Long-Term Care Home Deaths 
Two members of a long-term care home in the Haliburton, Kawartha, Pine Ridge District Health Unit have died after developing symptoms following a COVID-19 outbreak.
Fourteen staff members have tested positive for COVID-19, and 33 others at the home have developed symptoms.
