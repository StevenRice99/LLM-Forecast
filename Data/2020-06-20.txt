Title: Why did we hoard toilet paper? New research says it wasn't because of selfishness
Publisher: CTV News
Posted: 6 days ago
The study surveyed 996 people across 22 countries, including Canada, in late March 2020.
Conscientiousness (diligence and organization) was a strong predictor of toilet paper stockpiling.
Dependence and sentimentality also played a role in stockpiling habits.
Older respondents were more likely to purchase and stockpile toilet paper.
The study found no correlation between gender, political attitude, or local COVID-19 situation and hoarding habits.
