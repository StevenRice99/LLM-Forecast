Title: Not too late to get flu, COVID-19 shots before Christmas: experts
Publisher: Global News Toronto
Posted: 1 day ago
Relevant.

Article 2 of 7
Title: Many Canadians have had long COVID for almost 4 years. Researchers say there's hope
Publisher: CTV News
Posted: 1 day ago
11.7% of adult Canadians reported long-term symptoms lasting at least three months after COVID-19 infection (3.5 million people).
Long COVID affects many different systems in the body, with common symptoms including fatigue, brain fog, cardiovascular problems, and shortness of breath.
The risk of long COVID is still significant due to high Omicron variant infections.
Vaccinations may protect against long COVID too.
Researchers are studying various theories for causes of long COVID, including overactive inflammation, immune system dysregulation, microbiome disruption, endothelial cell damage, and viral persistence.
