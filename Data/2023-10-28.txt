Title: Effectiveness of the second COVID-19 booster against Omicron: a large-scale cohort study in Chile
Publisher: Nature.com
Posted: 1 day ago
A second mRNA booster dose provided high effectiveness against severe COVID-19 (74.2% to 86.3% against ICU admission, 79.3% to 90.8% against death).
The CCA vaccination scheme plus a second mRNA booster dose provided the highest protection against COVID-19-related deaths.
A study in Canada found that a fourth dose of an mRNA vaccine had 87% effectiveness against severe outcomes among residents in long-term care facilities aged 60 years or older.
Another study in Sweden found a decrease in short-term risk of death of about 70% among individuals older than 80 years who received a fourth dose of an mRNA vaccine.
These values are relevant for forecasting COVID-19 hospitalizations in Ontario, Canada.
