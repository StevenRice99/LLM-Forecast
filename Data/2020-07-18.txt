Title: How deadly is COVID-19 for children? Here's what we know
Publisher: CTV News
Posted: 4 days ago
In Canada, the death rate for children under 20 with COVID-19 is about 1 per 10,000 infected (0.013%).
Only one child has died from COVID-19 out of 7,888 cases in patients under 20.
Children are less likely to be hospitalized or receive ICU care compared to older patients (1.5% and 0.3%, respectively).
These numbers suggest a low risk for children contracting severe illness or hospitalization due to COVID-19.
