Publisher: CBC.ca

Key values:

Title: Unvaccinated people increase risk of COVID-19 infection among vaccinated: study - National
Publisher: Global News
Posted: 5 days ago
Unvaccinated people pose a threat to vaccinated individuals and contribute to increased hospitalization rates (attack rate among unvaccinated: 61% of hospitalizations).
Vaccination effectiveness, baseline immunity in unvaccinated people, and infection recovery rate are model parameters.
Mixing between vaccinated and unvaccinated populations affects risk; higher mixing leads to lower risk for unvaccinated individuals but increased risk for vaccinated ones.
Booster shots significantly protect against severe outcomes (hospitalization and death).
Public health officials emphasize collective action and vaccination as a means of protecting the community.

Article 6 of 7
Title: Province urges patience amid high demand for COVID-19 pill Paxlovid
Publisher: CBC.ca
Posted: 5 days ago
Paxlovid reduces the risk of hospitalization or death by 89% compared with a placebo.
The medication is most effective in those at highest risk of severe illness, including those 50 and older and anyone who is immunocompromised.
Approximately 50 to 60 people are being prescribed Paxlovid daily on P.E.I., which may indicate the potential demand for similar treatments in Ontario.
